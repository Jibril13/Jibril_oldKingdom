Hierarchy

Pharaoh

Pharaoh was the supreme leader of the lend for the old kingdom

Vizer

The Vizier was the pharaoh's chief advisor and depending on the circumstances was the high priest

Nobles

The Nobles was the people that govern Egypt some parts of egypt and had important positions in the government and economic, political power

Priests

The priests were the ones who had the role of keeping the gods happy, they were accomplishing by doing multiple different rituals.

scribbles

The people that could read and write was important to the old kingdom they were also known as scribbles and were positively viewed by the people

Workers

The workers were at the bottom of the hierarchy and were often getting paid with beer and bread but some workers were paid in goods
